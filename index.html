ALD: B2B Compliance Automation

First Automated Leasing Application in Industry

The Challenge:
EU money laundering regulations required manual review of every B2B application. 25+ different company types, multiple signers, days-long approval process.

Critical Decision: Existing requirements (from business workshops) were incomplete and non-compliant. Made call to start from scratch despite organizational discomfort.

  • Self-taught compliance law from legal publications
  • Completed money laundering certifications
  • Collaborated extensively with Chief Compliance Officer
  • Built comprehensive flows for all scenarios in Figma

Execution Reality: Limited development resources (junior frontend developer). Adapted approach to work within constraints while maintaining compliance standards.

Business Impact: 95% automation rate (industry first), processing time reduced from days to minutes, multiple FTEs freed for strategic work

Principle: Sometimes you have to rebuild foundations to achieve breakthrough results

ALD: motic Marketplace

Strategic Vision with Modular Execution

Industry Insight: Car dealers have unwritten rules about maximum discounts to protect margins. They prefer selling slow-sellers as "used cars" rather than revealing true discount potential.

Strategic Response:

  • Two-sided marketplace: dealers can offer deep discounts without margin exposure
  • Modular approach: components work standalone or as integrated platform
  • SaaS tool for dealers + consumer marketplace under our brand

Execution Challenges:

  • Business wanted to "cherry-pick" components vs. full vision
  • Fought for €20,000 research budget (internal experts insufficient)
  • Created modular architecture allowing gradual implementation

Strategic Learning: Large visions need decomposable execution - each module must deliver standalone value while building toward bigger goal

Transitioned to Steelbuy before full marketplace launch
